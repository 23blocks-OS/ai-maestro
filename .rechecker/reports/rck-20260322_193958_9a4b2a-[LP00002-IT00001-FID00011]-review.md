However, the primary issue here is the missing dependency for `setMarketplaceSkills` and `setLoadingMarketplace` which, while often stable, should be declared as dependencies for correctness.
**Fix**: Add `setMarketplaceSkills` and `setLoadingMarketplace` to the dependency array of the `useEffect` hook.

```typescript
  useEffect(() => {
```
