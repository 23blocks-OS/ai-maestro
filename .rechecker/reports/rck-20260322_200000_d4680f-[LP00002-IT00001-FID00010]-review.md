### BUG: Inconsistent `effectiveRef` usage in `handleScan`
**Severity**: medium
**Location**: `handleScan`
**Problem**: Inside the `handleScan` function, the `effectiveRef` variable is calculated using `ref.trim() || 'main'`. This `effectiveRef` is then used when calling `onSkillsFound` and setting `lastScannedRef`. However, when constructing the `body` for the `fetch` request, `ref.trim() || 'main'` is used directly in `JSON.stringify`. While these expressions are functionally equivalent, it's a minor inconsistency that could lead to subtle bugs if the logic for determining the effective ref were to change in one place but not the other. It's better to calculate `effectiveRef` once and reuse the variable.
**Fix**: Calculate `effectiveRef` once at the beginning of the `try` block and use this variable consistently for the `fetch` request body, `setLastScannedRef`, and `onSkillsFound`.

